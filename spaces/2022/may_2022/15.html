Mr. Morale & The Big Steppers is very different from all the other Kendrick albums we've seen, with all of it's pianos, foot tapping, and somewhat trap feeling throughout the album. i expected this change though, since he's hasn't released an album. for almost 5 years. but it seems as though anything Kendrick makes, it always sounds so good.

United In Grief, Rich Spirit, Purple Hearts, Silent Hill, N95, and Mirror are the tracks i've really liked from this album, with their lyrics that've been stuck in my head for a while, and instrumentals that fit the mood of what the track is trying to go for.
